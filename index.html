Can't decide on a movie?

Movieland is here to help! Each node represents a movie; hover to get more information and click to highlight movies with like actors or directors. Want to quickly see all the movies belonging to a certain rating distribution? Click on a bucket in the histogram to see all movies that score within that range. Want to look for movies based on actors or directors? Search for an actor/director or click on their node to highlight all of their works in the last decade.
